About Me

I am Ornela Hunda and this is my Portfolio website.

I was born in the south of Albania, located in southeastern of Europe. Since a child I was always fascinated by computers and technology but also I was very intrigued by the fact that how economy worked in different countries. I continued working on my childhood interests, while I pursued an education in Computer Science and Economics, at the University of Tirana.

Everyone has a lifetime dream and I have always pictured myself a successful person working in some office in New York. This is kind of impossible for a young person raised in Albania, but here I am halfway on my destination.

Never let you dreams die. If you have a heartbeat there is still time to make them come true.
